Host role — slotted label and help

Label and help are authored in Light DOM and projected into shadow slots. They remain light-tree nodes, so host.ariaLabelledByElements and host.ariaDescribedByElements work without cross-root shadow wiring. Prefer this pattern when consumers supply label and help markup.
