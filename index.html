Role on an inner shadow DOM element

The ARIA role lives on a native <input> (textfield, checkbox) or a <div role="..."> (progressbar, combobox) inside the shadow root. Label and description are also in the shadow DOM, with <slot name="label"> and <slot name="description"> for consumer-provided text. The role element is wired with aria-labelledby and aria-describedby using same-root IDs. The combobox uses Element.ariaActiveDescendantElement for its active option.
