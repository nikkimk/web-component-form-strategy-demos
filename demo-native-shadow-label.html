Shadow role — shadow DOM label and help

The ARIA role lives on an inner shadow DOM element. Label and help containers are also in the same shadow root, with their visible text slotted in from light DOM via <slot name="label"> and <slot name="help-text">. ariaLabelledByElements and ariaDescribedByElements are set on the shadow role element, pointing to the shadow label and help spans. Source and target share a shadow root, so no cross-root restriction applies.
